Living in a magnificent world with a myriad of people from multifarious backgrounds , hues and shades , it’s almost impossible to use one language for communicating with all the people. But imagine if there is one sweepingly powerful language which is not only a global language but also adroitly known by most of the people and most importantly connects the whole world in the most exquisite manner . Life will be easier ,connections so effortless and communication beautifully smooth ! This makes it imperative to learn English in order to meet and greet new people, become a part of a bigger community, bag your dream job, develop into an influential personality and most importantly feel effortlessly great every single day . But wait, is it too difficult to learn English? Most of the people in India find it difficult to master both written and speaking English. A few excel in writing and know all the rules of grammar but when it comes to speaking, people are shy, not so confident, stammer to present themselves. At, Gen-E we wholeheartedly help the people to get that extraordinary swoosh of confidence , impeccable knowledge and a push to make a difference . In our magnanimous universe of English - there is so much to learn from - from hitting back at the basics , to learning the most genuine tricks and tips to getting real time experience on how to communicate , write , speak , learn, read and express - the list is endless.
